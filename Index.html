Our Legacy

For more than 20 years, Saeed al Marri Laundry has been synonymous with quality and customer satisfaction. Our journey began with a commitment to provide Dubai residents and businesses with laundry services that exceed expectations.

Over the years, our dedication has made us a household name, known for our consistency, professionalism, and attention to detail.

Convenience

Customer Satisfaction

Sustainability

Saeed al Marri Laundry Services

  • Regular Laundry
    From everyday wear to special garments, trust us to clean, dry, and fold your clothes with care. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures a fresh and crisp finish every time.
  • Dry Cleaning
    For delicate fabrics and garments that require special attention, our dry-cleaning service guarantees a gentle yet thorough cleaning process, preserving the quality of your clothing.
  • Ironing and Pressing
    Say goodbye to wrinkles and creases. Our skilled team will expertly iron and press your clothes, leaving them looking sharp and professional.
  • Stain Removal
    Stubborn stains are no match for our stain removal experts. We employ advanced techniques and eco-friendly products to tackle even the toughest stains.
  • Specialty Items
    Whether it's your wedding gown, curtains, or linens, we have the expertise to clean and preserve your specialty items, ensuring they look their best.
  • Pickup and Delivery
    Enjoy the ultimate convenience with our pickup and delivery service. We'll collect your laundry from your doorstep and return it fresh and clean, saving you time and effort.
  • Eco-Friendly Practices
    We are committed to sustainability. Our eco-friendly detergents and responsible laundry practices minimize our environmental impact.
  • Corporate Services
    Partner with us for corporate laundry solutions. We offer specialized packages tailored to the needs of businesses, hotels, and restaurants.
  • Customized Solutions
    If you have a specific request or need, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Our team is dedicated to providing customized solutions to ensure your complete satisfaction.
